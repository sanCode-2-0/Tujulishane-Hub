What is the RMNCAH Coordination Hub?

The RMNCAH Coordination Hub is a national digital platform providing real-time visibility into program activities across Kenya. It maps who is working where and on what, helping reduce fragmentation, avoid duplication, and improve alignment of partner efforts.

Why It Matters

By improving coordination between partners, counties, and the Ministry of Health, the Hub ensures RMNCAH programs truly reach the communities that need them most. It accelerates decision-making, enhances transparency, and supports a more effective health system.
