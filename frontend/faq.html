ANSWER

The RMNCAH Coordination Hub is an official Ministry of Health digital platform designed to enhance visibility, coordination, and accountability among stakeholders implementing projects in Reproductive, Maternal, Newborn, Child, and Adolescent Health (RMNCAH). It enables partners and donors to register, submit projects, collaborate, and align efforts with national health priorities.

ANSWER

The platform streamlines coordination between the Ministry of Health, implementing partners, and donors. It ensures transparency in project implementation, minimizes duplication of efforts, and provides an up-to-date view of ongoing initiatives through interactive maps and project dashboards.
