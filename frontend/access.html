Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about RMNCAH coordination

Full FAQ Page

The RMNCAH Coordination Hub is an official Ministry of Health digital platform designed to enhance visibility, coordination, and accountability among stakeholders implementing projects in Reproductive, Maternal, Newborn, Child, and Adolescent Health (RMNCAH). It enables partners and donors to register, submit projects, collaborate, and align efforts with national health priorities.

Approved RMNCAH stakeholders including partner organizations (NGOs, CBOs, private sector implementers), donor organizations, and Ministry of Health (MOH) staff can register. Each account type requires verification and approval by the MOH admin to ensure authenticity and relevance.

To register, follow these simple steps:

  1. Click the Become a Member tab on this page.
  2. Fill in your Account Details (Name, Email, Organization, and Account Type).
  3. Click Next to proceed to Step 2: Supporting Documents (Optional, but recommended for faster approval).
  4. Submit your registration. Your credentials will be reviewed by the MOH team.

Uploading organizational registration certificates, MOUs with the Ministry of Health, introductory letters, or recent project briefs helps verify your organization's legitimacy. Supported formats include PDF, DOC/DOCX, and XLS/XLSX, with a maximum file size of 10MB per file.

Once submitted, your account goes through a two-step validation process by the MOH reviewer and administrator. This typically takes up to 12 hours. You will receive an email confirmation once your account has been approved or if additional information is required.
